Key Changes

  • Healthcare ITAs surged by 700%, reflecting a strong focus on healthcare professionals.
  • Program-Based draws dropped by 48.5%, with CEC seeing a 50% reduction.
  • No French-Speaking or Education draws in July, indicating a shift in priorities.

Key Insights

  • Over 50% of Express Entry's 2025 capacity utilized by July (39,200 for Category-Based, 40,000 for Program-Based).
  • Expect 38,000–40,000 additional ITAs by year-end, likely focusing on PNP and CEC.
  • The 1.6 coefficient accounts for family members in capacity estimates.
  • Some applications may be rejected or withdrawn, making the 1.6 coefficient a realistic estimate.
  • Healthcare draws dominated July with 4,000 ITAs, representing a 700% increase from June.
  • Program-Based draws declined significantly (-48.5%) as focus shifted to Category-Based selections.
  • French-Speaking draws remained inactive in July, suggesting targeted recruitment periods.
