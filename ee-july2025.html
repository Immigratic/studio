July 2025 Key Insights

Major Highlights

  • Healthcare Surge: Healthcare draws exploded from 500 to 4,000 ITAs (+700%), indicating urgent demand for healthcare professionals.
  • Program-Based Decline: Traditional program-based draws decreased by 48.5%, showing a strategic shift toward category-based selections.
  • CEC Reduction: Canadian Experience Class ITAs dropped by 50% (6,000 → 3,000), suggesting saturation or policy adjustment.
  • PNP Stability: Provincial Nominee Program maintained moderate activity with 558 ITAs, down 38.3% from June.

Positive Trends

  • Overall ITA count increased by 2.1% (+153 ITAs) despite program-based declines
  • Healthcare professionals received unprecedented opportunities
  • Category-based draws showed strong government commitment to sector-specific needs

Areas of Concern

  • No French-speaking draws for the second consecutive month
  • Significant reduction in general program-based opportunities
  • STEM, Trade, and Agriculture categories remained inactive
