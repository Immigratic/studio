Express Entry Canada 2025: Analytical Report

Data Source: ImmiWatch - Immigratic Data Studio (July 2025)

Overview

As of July 2025, a total of 49,403 Invitations to Apply (ITAs) have been issued through Canada's Express Entry system, divided into Category-Based and Program-Based draws.

Category-Based Draws

Total ITAs: 24,500

Annual Target: 62,000 Permanent Residents

Capacity Utilized (est.): ~39,200 (~63% of target)

Remaining Capacity: ~22,800 (~13,000 primary applicants)

Program-Based Draws

Total ITAs: 24,903

Annual Target: 89,000 Permanent Residents

Capacity Utilized (est.): ~40,000 (~45% of target)

Remaining Capacity: ~49,000 (~25,000 ITAs)

Key Insights

  • Over half of the annual Express Entry capacity has been utilized by July 2025.
  • IRCC is expected to issue approximately 38,000–40,000 additional ITAs by year-end, primarily through PNP and CEC pathways.
  • The 1.6 coefficient accounts for family members, with each ITA representing the primary applicant only.
  • Some applications may be rejected or withdrawn, making the 1.6 coefficient a realistic estimate for capacity planning.

Note: Capacities are based on the maximum annual permanent residency allocations, not just ITAs. Family members are included in IRCC’s final capacity counts.
